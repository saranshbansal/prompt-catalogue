## Why this exists

There are two types of people in the world:
1. Those who write prompts like they're sending a formal memo.
2. Those who treat prompts like ritual incantations.

This repository is for #2. It houses prompts that are crafted, optimized, annotated, and labeled with emotional intensity. Use them to prototype, ship, and meme your way to better outputs.

---

## What you'll find here
- Curated prompts organized by intent and power level.
- Templates for common workflows (summaries, refactors, role-playing interviews, data extraction).
- "Variants" that show escalation from polite to savage.
- Recommended model settings and expected output shapes.
- Small analytics: what worked, what flopped, and why.
